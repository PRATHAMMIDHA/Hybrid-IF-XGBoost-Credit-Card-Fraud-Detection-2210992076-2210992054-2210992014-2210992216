## 1. What is this project?
This project is an **End-to-End Machine Learning Research System** to detect credit card fraud. 

**The Problem:** In the real world, fraud is extremely rare. In our dataset of 284,807 transactions, only 492 are fraud (a ratio of 578 legitimate transactions to 1 fraud). Standard Artificial Intelligence fails at this because the AI can just guess "Legitimate" every time and be 99.8% accurate, even though it caught 0 frauds!

**The Solution:** We built a **Hybrid AI Model**. 
1. First, an **Isolation Forest** (an unsupervised AI) looks at transactions and gives them an "Anomaly Score" (how weird the transaction looks).
2. Then, an **XGBoost AI** (a supervised AI) looks at both the normal features AND the new Anomaly Score to make the final decision.
3. We then apply **Cost-Sensitive Thresholding**: We penalize the AI $500 if it misses a fraud, but only $10 if it makes a false alarm. This forces the AI to catch the maximum amount of fraud possible.
